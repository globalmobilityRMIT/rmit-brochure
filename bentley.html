  • Bentley University is one of the nation's leading business schools, dedicated to preparing a new kind of business leader, one with consolidated technical skills, a broad global perspective and the high ethical standards required to make a difference in an ever-changing world.

    Located on a classic New England campus close to Boston, Bentley University is a dynamic community of leaders, scholars and creative thinkers. There are approximately 4,200 undergraduates and 1,400 postgraduate students on campus.

    Located on 163 acres in Waltham, Massachusetts, the Bentley campus offers the best of both worlds: a safe, suburban campus with ready access to Boston, the largest college town in the country.

  • Waltham stretches along the Charles River and is commonly referred to as the “Watch City” because of its association with the watch industry. The Waltham Watch Company opened its factory in Waltham in 1854 and was the first company to make watches on an assembly line.

    Moody Street in downtown Waltham is called “Restaurant Row” because of the incredible variety of quality restaurants there. Downtown Waltham has a great atmosphere, with a bustling nightlife and a colourful range of shops.

  • Bentley’s on-campus activities include athletics, the arts, cultural events, volunteering and access to more than 100 student organisations.

Living

Accommodation

On-campus accommodation is guaranteed for all nominated exchange students. The Residence Life Office will do their best to place exchange students in the accommodation of their choice. Most exchange students are placed in shared rooms (singles are rarely available).

Off-campus housing is an option, but Bentley does not provide assistance in finding off-campus housing; they recommend that
exchange students live on campus due to the limited public transportation resources in the area.

Health insurance

All exchange students are required to purchase the Bentley
University student health insurance. Students will receive information about their health insurance electronically, and will be automatically enrolled in the insurance program so that it is in effect and students are covered when they arrive at Bentley.
